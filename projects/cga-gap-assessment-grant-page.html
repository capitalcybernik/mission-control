What is CMMC?

The Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) is a Department of Defense framework that requires all DoD contractors to meet specific cybersecurity standards. Without certification, companies cannot bid on or hold DoD contracts.

What is a SPRS score?

The Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS) score measures how well your company meets NIST SP 800-171 cybersecurity controls. It ranges from -203 to 110. Your gap assessment helps determine this score.

I am not sure if I need CMMC. Should I still apply?

If your company holds or plans to bid on any Department of Defense contract, you will likely need CMMC certification. The gap assessment will help you understand your requirements and current posture.
