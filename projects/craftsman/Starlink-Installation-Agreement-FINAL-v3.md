## 2. SERVICES INCLUDED

At each location, Capital Cyber will perform the following:

- **Site survey** to determine optimal Starlink dish placement with clear sky view
- **Physical installation** of the Starlink dish on the roof or exterior wall using appropriate mounting hardware
- **Cable routing** from the Starlink dish to the network equipment area (up to 150 ft per location)
- **Integration with existing WatchGuard firewall** including WAN interface configuration
- **Failover configuration** to enable automatic switchover between the primary ISP and Starlink in the event of an internet outage
- **Failover testing** to verify automatic switchover and switchback functionality
- **Speed and connectivity testing** to confirm stable Starlink operation
- **Documentation** of the installation, network configuration, and failover settings for the Client's records

## 11. IMPORTANT NOTES

- **Starlink requires a clear view of the sky.** Obstructions such as trees, buildings, or covered areas may require adjusted dish placement. Capital Cyber will determine the best available position during the site survey.
- **Starlink Business Priority includes:** Publicly routable IPv4 address, 24/7 prioritized support, Service Level Agreement (SLA), and priority data allocation.
- **No long term contracts with Starlink.** The Client may pause or cancel Starlink service at any time without penalty.
- **Failover behavior** depends on the WatchGuard firewall model and firmware version. Capital Cyber will configure failover using the best method supported by the Client's existing hardware.
