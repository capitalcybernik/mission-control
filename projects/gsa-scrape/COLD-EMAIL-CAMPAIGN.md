# Cold Email Campaign: GSA Manufacturing & Construction Contractors

## Campaign Overview
- **Target:** 360 manufacturing & construction companies with active GSA contracts
- **Markets:** NJ (40), NY (40), PA (51), CT (11), DE (4), VA (57), MD (40), DC (5), FL (81), AZ (23), NV (8)
- **Hook:** GSA just rolled out a new CMMC-like cybersecurity framework requiring CUI protection on contractor systems
- **Goal:** Book discovery calls → CMMC gap assessments → managed cybersecurity clients

## The Angle
GSA issued CIO-IT Security-21-112, a new framework requiring contractors handling CUI on GSA contracts to meet NIST SP 800-171 Rev 3 controls. Combined with DoD CMMC Phase 2 starting November 2026, any manufacturer or construction company doing government work now faces cybersecurity compliance from BOTH sides.

### Key Sources to Reference
- GSA Blog (Feb 12, 2026): https://www.gsa.gov/blog/2026/02/12/get-to-know-the-cybersecurity-maturity-model-certification
- GSA CUI Framework: CIO-IT Security-21-112 (NIST 800-171 Rev 3)
- Reuters (Feb 20, 2026): Small suppliers rethinking defense work due to compliance costs
- SBA Roundtable (March 12, 2026): CMMC Small Business Impacts
