# CMMC/Cybersecurity TikTok Hooks Library

20 scroll-stopping hooks for manufacturing and construction contractors, written in the viral LinkedIn pattern style.

## Fear/Urgency Hooks

1. **"The Pentagon just changed the rules."**
   - Perfect for CMMC Phase 2 announcements
   - Creates immediate authority and concern

2. **"Your GSA contract has a new requirement you don't know about."**
   - Targets government contractors directly
   - Implies insider knowledge

3. **"November 2026 is closer than you think."**
   - CMMC Phase 2 deadline urgency
   - Time pressure without being alarmist

4. **"127 contractors got certified. The rest lost their contracts."**
   - Social proof + fear of missing out
   - Real vs. fake consequences

5. **"The FBI just released the small business cybersecurity report."**
   - Authority source + newsworthiness
   - Perfect for recent FBI data

6. **"Defense contractors are about to face their biggest challenge yet."**
   - Industry-specific urgency
   - Builds curiosity about "the challenge"

7. **"Your competitor just got CMMC certified. You didn't."**
   - Direct competitive pressure
   - Personal relevance

8. **"One ransomware attack costs $500,000. CMMC compliance costs $15,000."**
   - Cost comparison shock value
   - Logical financial argument

## Educational/Discovery Hooks

9. **"Most contractors think CMMC is optional. They're wrong."**
   - Corrects common misconception
   - Sets up educational content

10. **"CMMC isn't cybersecurity. It's contract eligibility."**
    - Reframes the conversation
    - Business impact over tech specs

11. **"The difference between CMMC Level 1 and Level 2 will shock you."**
    - Clickbait style but delivers value
    - Educational angle

12. **"43% of cyberattacks target small manufacturers. Here's why."**
    - FBI statistic + educational promise
    - Industry-specific targeting

13. **"Your IT guy can't make you CMMC compliant. Here's what can."**
    - Challenges assumptions
    - Sets up solution positioning

14. **"NIST 800-171 has 110 controls. You only need to worry about 14."**
    - Simplifies overwhelming topic
    - Makes it approachable

## Insider Knowledge Hooks

15. **"Defense contractors who start CMMC now get first pick of contracts."**
    - Early mover advantage
    - Competitive intelligence

16. **"The DOD just published the Phase 2 timeline. Most contractors missed it."**
    - Insider information angle
    - Creates urgency to pay attention

17. **"Prime contractors are requiring CMMC from subs 6 months early."**
    - Supply chain pressure
    - Real market dynamics

18. **"Government auditors look for these 3 things first."**
    - Behind the scenes knowledge
    - Practical insider tips

## Success/Transformation Hooks

19. **"From $2M in contracts to $8M in 18 months. Here's what changed."**
    - Dramatic success story
    - Quantified transformation

20. **"This contractor went from audit failure to CMMC certified in 90 days."**
    - Transformation timeline
    - Proof that it's achievable

## Usage Notes

- Each hook should be followed by 5-6 slides that deliver on the promise
- Mix fear/urgency with educational content
- Always end with actionable CTA
- Rotate hooks to avoid audience fatigue
- Test performance and double down on winners

## Slide Structure Reminder

1. **Hook** (from this list)
2. **Problem** (elaborate on the pain point)
3. **Discovery** (reveal the insight/data)
4. **Transformation 1** (first benefit/solution element)
5. **Transformation 2** (second benefit/case study)
6. **CTA** (drive to capital-cyber.com or cybergrantsalliance.org)
