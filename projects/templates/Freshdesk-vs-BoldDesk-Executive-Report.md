# Freshdesk vs BoldDesk: Executive Decision Report

**Prepared by:** Capital Cyber
**Date:** March 3, 2026

---

## Overview

This report compares Freshdesk (by Freshworks) and BoldDesk (by Syncfusion) as helpdesk and ticketing platforms for our IT support operations. Both are cloud based, multi channel ticketing systems with automation, knowledge base, and reporting capabilities.

---

## Pricing Comparison (Per Agent, Per Month, Billed Annually)

| Tier | Freshdesk | BoldDesk |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Entry** | Free (limited) | $18 (Scale) |
| **Mid Tier** | $15 (Growth) | $35 (Momentum) |
| **Advanced** | $49 (Pro) | $49 (Enterprise) |
| **Enterprise** | $79 (Enterprise) | $49 (Enterprise, top tier) |

**Cost for a 3 agent team (mid tier):**
Freshdesk Growth: **$45/mo** ($540/yr)
BoldDesk Momentum: **$105/mo** ($1,260/yr)

**Cost for a 3 agent team (advanced):**
Freshdesk Pro: **$147/mo** ($1,764/yr)
BoldDesk Enterprise: **$147/mo** ($1,764/yr)

**Winner: Freshdesk** at entry and mid tier pricing. At the advanced tier, pricing is identical.

## Strengths

### Freshdesk
- **Mature ecosystem** with a large marketplace of 1,000+ integrations
- **Free tier** available for very small teams or testing
- **Strong brand recognition** and extensive documentation
- **Freshworks suite** integration (Freshservice, Freshsales, Freshchat) if future expansion is planned
- **Larger community** for support and third party resources

### BoldDesk
- **Better value at the advanced tier** ($49 gets you what Freshdesk charges $79 for)
- **AI features included** with credits on Momentum and Enterprise plans (no expensive add on)
- **SLA management on all plans** (Freshdesk locks this behind the $49 Pro plan)
- **Native Microsoft Teams integration** (important for M365 environments)
- **Simpler pricing** with fewer gotchas and feature lockouts
- **HIPAA compliance at $49** vs Freshdesk's $79
- **Built by Syncfusion**, a well established software company (not a startup)

## Recommendation

**For a lean IT support operation (3 to 5 agents) managing client tickets:**

**BoldDesk Enterprise at $49/agent/mo** delivers the best overall value. It includes SLA management, HIPAA compliance, agent shifts, AI copilot credits, native Teams integration, and multi brand support, all features that Freshdesk locks behind its $79 Enterprise plan. The annual cost difference for a 3 agent team at equivalent feature parity is approximately **$1,080/yr saved with BoldDesk**.

**Choose Freshdesk if:**
- You need the free tier to start with zero budget
- You plan to expand into Freshworks' broader suite (CRM, ITSM)
- You require a specific marketplace integration only available on Freshdesk

**Choose BoldDesk if:**
- You want the most features per dollar at the mid to enterprise tier
- Native Microsoft Teams integration is important
- You want AI capabilities included without expensive add ons
- HIPAA compliance is needed without paying top tier pricing
