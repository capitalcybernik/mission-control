## Key Stats

- **17 construction/infrastructure** solicitations requiring CMMC
- **14 manufacturing/defense parts** solicitations requiring CMMC
- **5+ IT/services/R&D** solicitations requiring CMMC
- **Most common requirement:** CMMC Level 2 (Self Assessment)
- **Growing trend:** Several solicitations now require Level 2 C3PAO (third party assessment)
- **Biggest agency:** US Army Corps of Engineers (dominant in construction CMMC requirements)
- **Key escalation:** SOF Tactical Equipment Facility starts at Level 1, escalates to Level 2 by Oct 2026 and Level 3 by Oct 2027
