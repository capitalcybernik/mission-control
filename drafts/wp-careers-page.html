Why Capital Cyber

Most IT companies bolt on security as an afterthought. We built Capital Cyber the other way around. We're a cybersecurity firm that provides IT, not an IT company that dabbles in security.

Our founder started Capital Cyber after watching a $500K ransomware attack destroy a business. That experience made one thing clear: small and mid sized companies deserve the same level of protection as the big guys.

With 24 years of combined IT experience, 100% client retention, and a team that genuinely cares about protecting people, we're building something different. We work remotely, we stay lean, and we focus on doing excellent work for every client we take on.
