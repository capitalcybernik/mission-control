The Big 5 Controls Every Carrier Requires

These are the non-negotiables. If you're missing any of them, your premium goes up, your coverage goes down, or your claim gets denied.

1

Multi-Factor Authentication

Required on email, VPN, remote access, and admin portals. If someone steals a password, MFA stops them at the door.

2

Endpoint Detection & Response

Not antivirus. EDR watches what programs actually do on your computers and stops threats based on behavior, not just known signatures.

3

Immutable Backups

Backups that ransomware can't encrypt or delete. Stored offsite, tested regularly, and ready to restore your business in hours, not weeks.

4

Security Awareness Training

Regular training with simulated phishing tests. Most attacks start with someone clicking the wrong link. Training is how you stop that.

5

Patch Management

Every device, every application, kept current. Unpatched software is an open door with a welcome mat for attackers.
