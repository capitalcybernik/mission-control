# Research Report: Manufacturing Innovation Organizations Similar to NextCorps
## National Partnership Opportunities for Cyber Grants Alliance

**Date:** February 14, 2026
**Prepared for:** Cyber Grants Alliance

---

## Executive Summary

This report identifies 10 organizations across the United States that are similar to NextCorps (nextcorps.org) - manufacturing innovation organizations that serve manufacturing companies, offer grants/funding programs, and help with commercialization, business growth, and innovation. These organizations would benefit from partnering with Cyber Grants Alliance to promote cybersecurity grants to their manufacturing clients.

---

## Summary and Recommendations

### Top Partnership Priorities:

1. **FuzeHub (NY)** - Strongest overall fit with innovation fund experience and statewide reach
2. **FloridaMakes** - Already has cybersecurity programs; large manufacturer network
3. **CONNSTEP (CT)** - Explicitly offers cybersecurity services; strong compliance focus
4. **Catalyst Connection (PA)** - Active grant programs for advanced manufacturing
5. **GENEDGE (VA)** - Defense-heavy state; strong government contractor focus

### Regional Coverage:
- **Northeast:** FuzeHub (NY), CONNSTEP (CT), Polaris MEP (RI), Catalyst Connection (PA)
- **Southeast:** FloridaMakes
- **Midwest:** Missouri Enterprise
- **West:** Manufacturer's Edge (CO), Utah-MEP, New Mexico MEP
- **Mid-Atlantic:** GENEDGE (VA)

### Key Partnership Benefits:
All 10 organizations:
- Have established relationships with manufacturers
- Offer grant/funding assistance programs
- Serve small and medium manufacturers (the target for many cybersecurity grants)
- Have demonstrated economic impact and trust in their regions
- Work with defense and critical infrastructure manufacturers who need cybersecurity
- Would benefit from offering additional funding resources to their clients

### Next Steps:
1. Contact Center Directors at top 5 organizations
2. Present Cyber Grants Alliance value proposition
3. Offer educational webinars on cybersecurity grants for manufacturers
4. Develop co-branded materials explaining grant opportunities
5. Track referrals and successful grant applications
